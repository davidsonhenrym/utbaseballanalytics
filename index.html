UTScore percentiles each metric within a player's position group, weights them, and ranks the field on a 0–100 scale.
How UTScore works: each selected metric is converted to a percentile within the player's position group (or whole pool, in "Vs All" mode), multiplied by your weights, and summed to a 0–100 composite. Lower-is-better metrics (ERA, FIP, K%, etc.) are inverted automatically. Only players meeting the minimum sample threshold are scored, so small samples don't distort the field. Adjust weights live; rankings recompute instantly.
